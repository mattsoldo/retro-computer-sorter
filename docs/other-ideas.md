# Other ideas — future iterations

- **Decimals mode:** Introduce tenths / hundredths bins with sports/physics facts (batting averages, g-forces).
- **Boss rounds:** Every 5 levels a "boss number" falls — an eight-digit spec that requires the player to identify the leading digit's place value under 4 seconds.
- **Operator challenges:** Two numbers collide mid-air and the player must sort the sum/product.
- **Custom object packs:** Space probes (distance from sun), dinosaurs (years ago, weight in kg), national parks (acreage).
- **Two-player co-op:** One player steers, the other fast-drops — race to sort 20 in under a minute.
- **Accessibility pass:** Colour-blind safe bin palette, high-contrast toggle, dyslexia-friendly font option.
- **Tutorial mode:** A guided first-run walkthrough with the number hovering and arrows appearing on screen.
- **Achievement / badge system:** "First MHz", "Megabyte Master", "Pac-Man Perfectionist" — Steam-style popups.
- **Touch controls:** Drag-to-steer for tablet play (works without keyboard).
- **Leaderboard sync:** Optional nickname + score submission to a shared online board via serverless function.
